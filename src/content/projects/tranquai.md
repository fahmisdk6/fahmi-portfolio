AI-powered meditation app that creates personalized meditation sessions. Users specify their meditation goal, select a preferred voice, and the app generates a custom experience tailored to their needs — whether for relaxation before meetings, focus during study, or sleep preparation.

## Key Features

- Unlimited customization — adjust purpose and duration for individualized sessions
- Voice selection — choose from lifelike, soothing narrators
- Quick activation — minimal setup required to begin meditating
